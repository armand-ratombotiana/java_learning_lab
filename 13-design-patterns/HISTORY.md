# History of Design Patterns

## Origins

### 1977-1979: Christopher Alexander
- Architect who wrote "A Pattern Language"
- Created pattern concept for building architecture
- Key idea: Solutions to recurring problems in environments

### 1987: Ward Cunningham and Kent Beck
- Applied pattern ideas to software
- "Using Pattern Languages for Object-Oriented Programs"

### 1994: Design Patterns (Gang of Four)
- Erich Gamma, Richard Helm, Ralph Johnson, John Vlissides
- Published "Design Patterns: Elements of Reusable Object-Oriented Software"
- 23 classic patterns cataloged

## Pattern Evolution
