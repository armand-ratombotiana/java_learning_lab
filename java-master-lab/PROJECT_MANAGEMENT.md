## 🚀 Risk Management

### Identified Risks

#### Technical Risks
- **Risk**: Complexity of advanced topics
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Impact**: High
- **Mitigation**: Phased approach, clear specifications

#### Schedule Risks
- **Risk**: Timeline delays
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Impact**: High
- **Mitigation**: Buffer time, regular reviews

#### Resource Risks
- **Risk**: Insufficient resources
- **Probability**: Low
- **Impact**: High
- **Mitigation**: Flexible team structure

#### Quality Risks
- **Risk**: Quality degradation
- **Probability**: Low
- **Impact**: High
- **Mitigation**: Comprehensive testing, code review

### Risk Response Plan
1. **Identify**: Monitor for risks
2. **Assess**: Evaluate probability and impact
3. **Respond**: Implement mitigation
4. **Monitor**: Track risk status
5. **Review**: Adjust as needed
