## 🎪 Interview Day Preparation

### Day Before
```
❌ Don't: Study new material, stay up late
✅ Do: Review previous problems, get good sleep
✅ Do: Test video/audio setup, prepare questions
✅ Do: Have water and light snack nearby
```

### Day Of (30 minutes before)
```
✅ Prepare whiteboard/pen (visible on camera)
✅ Have glass of water ready
✅ Close all distractions
✅ Use bathroom before starting
✅ Smile (it comes through in voice tone)
```

### During Interview
```
Coding Round:
├─ Ask clarifying questions (2 min)
├─ Discuss approach before coding (3 min)
├─ Code solution (15 min)
├─ Trace through examples (3 min)
└─ Discuss optimizations (2 min)

System Design Round:
├─ Ask scope questions (3 min)
├─ Draw simple architecture (5 min)
├─ Deep dive on bottlenecks (15 min)
├─ Discuss tradeoffs (10 min)
└─ Show you can adapt to feedback (7 min)
```

### After Interview
```
✅ Do: Send thank you email (within 1 hour)
✅ Do: Write down feedback while fresh
✅ Do: Identify what you struggled with
✅ Do: Continue prepping for other rounds

❌ Don't: Assume you failed (interviews are hard)
❌ Don't: Give up if one interview goes poorly
❌ Don't: Over-analyze what they think
```
