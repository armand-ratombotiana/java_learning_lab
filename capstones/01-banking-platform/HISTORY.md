# History: Banking Platform

- **1970s**: First electronic banking systems (mainframe-based, batch processing)
- **1990s**: Online banking emerges; customer-facing web portals
- **2000s**: Core banking platforms shift to SOA; payment gateways (PayPal, 2000)
- **2010s**: Fintech revolution — Stripe (2010), Square, modern digital banks (Chime, Revolut)
- **2020s**: Open banking APIs (PSD2), real-time payments (FedNow, UPI), cloud-native banking cores

This capstone reflects the 2020s generation: microservice-based, event-driven, containerized, with fraud ML and real-time processing.
