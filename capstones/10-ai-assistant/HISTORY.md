# History: AI Assistant

- **1966**: ELIZA (MIT) — first chatbot, simple pattern matching
- **1995**: ALICE — AIML-based chatbot, won Loebner Prize
- **2010**: Siri (Apple), Google Now — voice assistants with limited capabilities
- **2014**: Alexa (Amazon), Cortana (Microsoft) — smart home assistants
- **2020**: GPT-3 — LLM-based chat capabilities emerge
- **2022**: ChatGPT — mainstream AI assistant with broad capabilities
- **2023**: GPT-4, Claude, Gemini — tool calling, plugins, multimodal
- **2024-2025**: Agentic AI — autonomous task execution, multi-agent systems, memory, planning
