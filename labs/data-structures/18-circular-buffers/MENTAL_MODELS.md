# Mental Models

## 1. The Round Table Model
Think of a round table with N seats. A waiter places dishes at the next empty seat (tail). A diner takes dishes from the next occupied seat (head). When they reach the end, they continue from the start.

## 2. The Clock Model
The buffer is like a clock face. The head hand shows where to read next. The tail hand shows where to write next. They chase each other around the clock.

## 3. The Conveyor Belt Model
A conveyor belt with N bins. New items go into the next empty bin. Items are removed from the next full bin. The belt moves forward; when it reaches the end, it wraps to the beginning.
