# Why Van Emde Boas Tree Exists

## The Problem

Traditional data structures often fail to meet the demands of modern computing. As datasets grow to millions and billions of elements, limitations become critical bottlenecks.

## Limitations of Simpler Approaches

Standard implementations degrade to linear time in worst-case scenarios. Naive approaches waste memory and cannot scale.

## The Gap That Van Emde Boas Tree Fills

Van Emde Boas Tree addresses these limitations with guaranteed performance bounds, memory efficiency, scalability, and practical usability.

## Design Philosophy

The design reflects that mathematical optimality is achievable through careful algorithm design, trade-offs must be explicitly managed, and practical performance matters as much as asymptotic guarantees.
