# History of Spatial Data Structures

- **1974**: K-d tree invented by Jon Louis Bentley
- **1975**: Point quadtree by Raphael Finkel and Jon Bentley
- **1984**: R-tree by Antonin Guttman
- **1989**: R*-tree by Beckmann et al.
- **1990s**: Spatial indexing in GIS systems
- **2000s**: GPU-accelerated spatial queries
- **2010s**: 3D spatial structures for VR/AR
- **2020s**: Learned spatial indexes
