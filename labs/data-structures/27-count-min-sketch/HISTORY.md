# History of Count-Min Sketch

## Origins

The conceptual foundations emerged from research in algorithms and data structures.

## Key Milestones

1. **Theoretical Foundations**: Mathematical principles and complexity bounds established.
2. **First Implementations**: Practical feasibility demonstrated.
3. **Optimization and Refinement**: Constant factors improved for real-world use.
4. **Widespread Adoption**: Popularity in production systems.

## Evolution

Theoretical foundations in 1980s-1990s, widespread adoption in 2000s, continued refinement from 2010s to present.

## Modern Era

Today it is a standard tool with implementations in major programming languages.
