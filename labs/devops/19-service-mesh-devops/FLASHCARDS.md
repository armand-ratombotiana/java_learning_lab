# Service Mesh DevOps Flashcards

## Card 1
Q: What is Service Mesh DevOps?
A: Istio/Consul service mesh operations, canary deployments, traffic shifting, and observability.

## Card 2
Q: What are the key components?
A: Core services, configuration management, security controls, and monitoring integration.

## Card 3
Q: How does it ensure security?
A: Through authentication, authorization, encryption, audit logging, and secret management.

## Card 4
Q: What monitoring capabilities exist?
A: Health checks, metrics collection, logging, alerting, and dashboard integration.

## Card 5
Q: How does it handle failures?
A: Through graceful degradation, retry logic, circuit breakers, and automated recovery.

## Card 6
Q: What are the deployment patterns?
A: Blue-green deployments, canary releases, rolling updates, and feature flags.

## Card 7
Q: How does it integrate with CI/CD?
A: Through pipeline integration, automated testing, deployment hooks, and environment management.

## Card 8
Q: What best practices apply?
A: Version control, automated testing, least privilege, monitoring, and documentation.

## Card 9
Q: How do you troubleshoot issues?
A: Check logs, verify configuration, test connectivity, review metrics, examine errors.

## Card 10
Q: What scaling considerations exist?
A: Horizontal scaling, caching, load balancing, resource limits, and database sharding.
