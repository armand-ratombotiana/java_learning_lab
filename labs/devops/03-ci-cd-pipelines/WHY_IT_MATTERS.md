# Why CI/CD Matters

CI/CD is the backbone of modern software delivery:

- **Speed**: Deploy multiple times per day (vs monthly releases).
- **Quality**: Automated testing catches regressions immediately.
- **Reliability**: Consistent deployment process reduces human error.
- **Developer Productivity**: Automate repetitive tasks; focus on feature development.
- **Risk Reduction**: Small, frequent deployments reduce blast radius.
- **Compliance**: Auditable, repeatable deployment process for regulatory requirements.

Companies practicing CI/CD deploy 208x more frequently, with 106x faster lead time and 7x lower change failure rate (DORA State of DevOps).
