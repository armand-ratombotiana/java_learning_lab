# Reflection: GitOps & ArgoCD

## Key Takeaways
1. Git as the single source of truth transforms how we manage infrastructure
2. Pull-based deployments are more secure than push-based deployments
3. Self-healing infrastructure reduces operational toil
4. ApplicationSets dramatically simplify multi-cluster management
5. GitOps enables developer self-service without sacrificing control

## Questions to Consider
1. How would GitOps change your current deployment workflow?
2. What security concerns does pull-based deployment address?
3. How would you handle secrets in a GitOps workflow?
4. What is the right balance between automation and manual approval?
5. How would you migrate from traditional CI/CD to GitOps?

## Personal Application
Think about your current infrastructure and deployment processes. Identify areas where GitOps principles could improve reliability, security, or velocity. Consider the cultural changes needed to adopt GitOps in your organization.
