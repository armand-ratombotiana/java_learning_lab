# Secrets Management Interview Questions

## Junior
1. What is a secret and why is management important?
2. What problems does Vault solve?
3. How do you read a secret from Vault?

## Mid-Level
4. Explain Vault's seal/unseal mechanism and why it exists.
5. How do Vault policies work? Give an example.
6. What are dynamic secrets and how do they improve security?
7. How does Vault integrate with Kubernetes?

## Senior
8. Design a Vault architecture for a multi-region, HA deployment.
9. How do you rotate secrets without application downtime?
10. Compare Vault with cloud-native secrets managers (AWS Secrets Manager, GCP Secret Manager).
11. How do you handle secrets in a CI/CD pipeline securely?

## Principal/Architect
12. Design a zero-trust secrets management strategy for a global enterprise.
13. How do you implement secrets management compliance (SOC2, PCI-DSS, HIPAA)?
14. Compare Vault Enterprise vs open-source for large-scale deployments.
15. How do you manage secrets across multi-cloud and on-premises environments?
