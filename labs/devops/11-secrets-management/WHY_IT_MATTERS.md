# Why Secrets Management Matters

Secrets management is critical for security and compliance:

- **Security**: Prevent credential theft; limit blast radius with dynamic secrets.
- **Compliance**: SOC2, HIPAA, PCI-DSS require secrets management controls (audit, encryption, rotation).
- **Zero Trust**: No static credentials; every access is authenticated and authorized.
- **Operational Efficiency**: Automated rotation eliminates manual password change processes.
- **Developer Velocity**: Self-service secret access without waiting for operations.
- **Multi-Cloud**: Consistent secrets management across AWS, GCP, Azure, and on-premises.

Vault is the industry standard for secrets management (CNCF project). Data breaches involving credential theft cost companies $4.5M+ on average (IBM 2024).
