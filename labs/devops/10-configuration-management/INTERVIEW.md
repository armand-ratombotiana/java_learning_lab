# Configuration Management Interview Questions

## Junior
1. What is configuration management and why use it?
2. What is idempotency? Give an example.
3. How do you run an Ansible playbook?

## Mid-Level
4. Compare Ansible, Puppet, and Chef.
5. How do you handle secrets in Ansible?
6. What is the difference between Ansible roles and playbooks?
7. How do you structure a large Ansible project?

## Senior
8. Design a configuration management strategy for 5000+ servers.
9. How do you integrate Ansible with Terraform for complete infrastructure automation?
10. How do you handle Windows and Linux hosts in the same Ansible environment?
11. What strategies do you use for zero-downtime updates with CM tools?

## Principal/Architect
12. Design a self-service CM platform for multiple development teams.
13. How does CM fit into a GitOps and immutable infrastructure strategy?
14. Compare CM tools with container orchestration for application deployment.
15. How do you enforce compliance across heterogeneous infrastructure using CM?
