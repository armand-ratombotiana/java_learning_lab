# GitOps Interview Questions

## Junior
1. What is GitOps and why use it?
2. How does GitOps differ from traditional deployment?
3. What is ArgoCD?

## Mid-Level
4. Explain the GitOps reconciliation loop.
5. How does self-healing work in GitOps?
6. Compare ArgoCD and Flux.
7. How do you manage secrets in a GitOps workflow?

## Senior
8. Design a GitOps workflow for a multi-team, multi-cluster environment.
9. How do you handle rollbacks in GitOps? Walk through the process.
10. How do you integrate CI pipelines with GitOps CD?
11. What strategies do you use for progressive delivery with GitOps?

## Principal/Architect
12. Design a GitOps platform for 200+ microservices across 10 clusters and 3 regions.
13. How do you implement policy-as-code and compliance in GitOps?
14. Compare GitOps vs traditional CI/CD for a regulated industry deployment.
15. How do you manage GitOps at scale with thousands of applications?
