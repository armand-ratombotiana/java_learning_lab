# Monitoring & Logging Interview Questions

## Junior
1. What is the difference between monitoring and observability?
2. How do you view container logs?
3. What is a metric and what are common metric types?

## Mid-Level
4. Explain how Prometheus scrape and storage works.
5. How do you design a Grafana dashboard for a microservice?
6. What is the ELK stack and how does it handle log ingestion?
7. How do you configure alerting rules?

## Senior
8. How do you handle high cardinality metrics in Prometheus?
9. Design a multi-cluster observability strategy.
10. How do you implement log sampling without losing critical events?
11. Explain the trade-offs between pull and push monitoring models.

## Principal/Architect
12. Design an observability platform for 1000+ microservices across multiple regions.
13. How do you implement OpenTelemetry across an organization?
14. Compare Prometheus + Thanos vs Grafana Mimir vs Datadog.
15. How do you manage observability costs at scale?
