# Why Monitoring & Logging Matters

Observability is critical for operating distributed systems:

- **Uptime & Reliability**: Detect and respond to issues before users are impacted.
- **Performance Optimization**: Identify bottlenecks, plan capacity, measure SLOs/SLIs.
- **Root Cause Analysis**: Logs + metrics + traces = faster incident resolution.
- **Business Insights**: Understand user behavior, feature adoption, and revenue impact.
- **Compliance**: Audit logs for regulatory requirements (SOC2, HIPAA, PCI-DSS).
- **Cost Optimization**: Monitor resource utilization and identify waste.

Modern DevOps practices require comprehensive observability. The ELK stack and Prometheus are CNCF-graduated projects trusted by organizations worldwide.
