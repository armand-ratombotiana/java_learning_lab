# Container Orchestration Interview Questions

## Junior
1. What is auto-scaling and why is it important?
2. What probes does Kubernetes support?
3. How do you perform a rolling update?

## Mid-Level
4. Explain the HPA algorithm and stabilization window.
5. How do PDBs work and why are they important?
6. What are the deployment strategies available in K8s?
7. How do you configure resource requests and limits?

## Senior
8. Design an auto-scaling strategy for a latency-sensitive web service.
9. How would you implement blue/green deployments without service mesh?
10. Explain the interaction between HPA, cluster autoscaler, and VPA.
11. How do you handle canary deployments with progressive delivery?

## Principal/Architect
12. Design a multi-region orchestration strategy with disaster recovery.
13. How do you implement cost-optimized auto-scaling across spot/preemptible instances?
14. Compare event-driven auto-scaling (KEDA) vs metric-based (HPA).
