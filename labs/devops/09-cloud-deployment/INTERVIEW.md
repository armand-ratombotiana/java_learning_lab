# Cloud Deployment Interview Questions

## Junior
1. What are the benefits of deploying to the cloud?
2. How do you connect kubectl to a cloud K8s cluster?
3. What is the difference between IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS?

## Mid-Level
4. Compare EKS, GKE, and AKS managed Kubernetes services.
5. How do you secure a cloud K8s cluster?
6. What is the 12-factor app methodology?
7. How do you handle secrets in cloud deployments?

## Senior
8. Design a multi-region deployment architecture for high availability.
9. How do you optimize cloud costs across development and production?
10. What strategies do you use for cloud migration (lift-and-shift vs re-architecture)?
11. How do you implement disaster recovery for cloud-native applications?

## Principal/Architect
12. Design a multi-cloud strategy avoiding vendor lock-in while leveraging managed services.
13. How do you implement governance and compliance across 100+ cloud accounts?
14. Compare serverless (Lambda/Cloud Functions) vs container-based deployment.
15. How do you build a cloud platform engineering team and toolchain?
