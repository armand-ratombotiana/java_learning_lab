# CAP Theorem: Reflection

## Key Takeaways
- CAP is about tradeoffs, not absolutes
- Network partitions are inevitable in distributed systems
- Different use cases justify different CAP choices
- Modern systems increasingly use hybrid approaches

## Questions to Consider
1. How does your current system handle partitions?
2. What consistency guarantees do your users actually need?
3. Could eventual consistency solve your scalability problems?
4. Are you over-engineering for consistency you don't need?

## Personal Notes
- CAP theorem is often misunderstood as rigid when it's really a framework for thinking
- The most important lesson: acknowledge tradeoffs explicitly
- Real systems often combine CP and AP for different operations
