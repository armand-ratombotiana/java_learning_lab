# Distributed Consensus: Reflection

## Key Insights
- Consensus is the foundation of reliable distributed systems
- Raft's understandability was the key design goal
- FLP impossibility forces practical compromises
- Leader-based algorithms dominate production systems

## Questions
1. Why is Raft more popular than Paxos in industry?
2. Could your system work without consensus?
3. What's the cost of consensus in your infrastructure?
4. How do you verify your consensus implementation?

## Personal Notes
- Consensus seems abstract but powers most distributed databases
- Implementing Raft is the best way to understand it
- The tradeoff between simplicity and performance is real
