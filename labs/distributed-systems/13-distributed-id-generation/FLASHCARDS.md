# Flashcards — Distributed ID Generation

1. Q: Snowflake bit size? A: 64 bits
2. Q: UUID v7 bits? A: 128 bits
3. Q: ULID encoding? A: Crockford Base32
4. Q: Snowflake IDs per ms per node? A: 4096
5. Q: ULID characters? A: 26
6. Q: UUID v7 version bits? A: 0111 (7)
7. Q: Snowflake timestamp bits? A: 41
8. Q: Snowflake worker bits? A: 10 (1024 nodes)
9. Q: ULID timestamp characters? A: 10
10. Q: Snowflake epoch? A: Custom (not Unix epoch)
