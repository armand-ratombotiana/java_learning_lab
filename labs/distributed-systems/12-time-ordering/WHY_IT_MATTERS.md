# Why Time Ordering Matters

1. **Database Consistency**: Causal consistency without proper ordering
2. **Conflict Resolution**: Smart merge strategies for concurrent edits
3. **Debugging**: Distributed tracing relies on correct ordering
4. **Financial Systems**: Trade ordering determines pricing
5. **Event Sourcing**: Correct replay requires proper ordering
6. **Consensus Protocols**: Paxos/Raft use logical time
7. **Bottom Line**: Every distributed system must handle time
