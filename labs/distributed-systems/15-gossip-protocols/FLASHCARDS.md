# Flashcards — Gossip Protocols

1. Q: What is the primary purpose? A: Provide gossip-protocols at scale
2. Q: Key components? A: Client, coordinator, workers, storage
3. Q: Failure handling? A: Retry, failover, circuit breakers
4. Q: Consistency model? A: Depends on configuration
5. Q: Scaling approach? A: Add nodes, partition data
6. Q: Monitoring metrics? A: Latency, throughput, errors, saturation
7. Q: Deployment model? A: Distributed cluster
8. Q: Communication protocol? A: Typically TCP/HTTP
9. Q: Data storage? A: Various backends supported
10. Q: Security model? A: Authentication, authorization, encryption
