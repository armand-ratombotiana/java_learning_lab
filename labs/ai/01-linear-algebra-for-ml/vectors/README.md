# Vectors in Machine Learning

Welcome to the atomic mastery lab for **Vectors**. This lab is part of the AI Academy's Linear Algebra foundations.

## 🧠 What You Will Master
- Geometric vs. Algebraic interpretation of vectors.
- Vector operations: Addition, Scalar Multiplication, Dot Product.
- The mathematical significance of the Dot Product (Cosine Similarity).
- Vector norms (L1, L2, L-infinity) and their role in regularization.
- Implementing Vector operations from scratch in Java.
