# Word2Vec & Embeddings

Welcome to the atomic mastery lab for **Word2Vec**. This lab is part of the AI Academy's Embeddings module.

## 🧠 What You Will Master
- The concept of Semantic Vector Spaces (Embeddings).
- Why One-Hot Encoding fails for Natural Language Processing.
- The Continuous Bag of Words (CBOW) and Skip-Gram architectures.
- Negative Sampling optimization.
- Implementing a basic Word2Vec model in Java.
