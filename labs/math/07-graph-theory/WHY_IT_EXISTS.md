# Why Graph Theory Exists

Graph theory was born from a practical question: Can you walk through Königsberg crossing each bridge exactly once?

## Core Motivations

- **Networks**: roads, railways, telephone lines, the internet
- **Relationships**: friendships, citations, hyperlinks
- **Hierarchies**: organizational charts, file systems, taxonomies
- **Flows**: water, electricity, data, traffic
- **Scheduling**: project dependencies, course prerequisites

Graphs are the natural mathematical model whenever entities connect to each other.
