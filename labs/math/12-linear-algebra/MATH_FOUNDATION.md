# Math Foundation for Linear Algebra

## Prerequisite Knowledge

### 1. Basic Algebra
- Variables, expressions, equations
- Functions and function notation
- Polynomial arithmetic

### 2. Calculus Fundamentals
- Derivatives and rates of change
- Basic integration concepts
- Series and sequences

### 3. Linear Algebra (where applicable)
- Vectors and matrices
- Systems of linear equations
- Determinants and eigenvalues

### 4. Probability Basics
- Random variables
- Probability distributions
- Expectation and variance

### 5. Proof Techniques
- Direct proof
- Proof by contradiction
- Induction

## Self-Assessment

1. Can you solve a system of two linear equations?
2. Can you compute the derivative of a polynomial?
3. Can you explain what a function's minimum means?
4. Can you work with summations and series notation?

## Recommended Review

- Khan Academy: Algebra, Calculus, Linear Algebra
- MIT OpenCourseWare: relevant mathematics courses
- Any undergraduate mathematics textbook covering the prerequisites
