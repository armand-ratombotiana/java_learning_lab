# Derivatives

Welcome to the atomic mastery lab for **Derivatives**. This lab is part of the Math Academy's Calculus module.

## 🧠 What You Will Master
- The concept of Limits and instantaneous rate of change.
- The Power Rule, Product Rule, and Quotient Rule.
- The Chain Rule (crucial for Backpropagation).
- Partial Derivatives in multivariable functions.
- Implementing numerical differentiation in Java.

## 📂 Lab Structure
1. [THEORY.md](./THEORY.md) - Intuition behind rates of change.
2. [MATH_FOUNDATION.md](./MATH_FOUNDATION.md) - Formal definitions and rules of differentiation.
3. [CODE_DEEP_DIVE.md](./CODE_DEEP_DIVE.md) - Pure Java implementation of numerical differentiation.
