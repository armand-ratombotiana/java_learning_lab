# Flashcards: Algebra

## Category 1: Linear Equations

### Card 1
**Q:** Solve: 2x + 5 = 13
**A:** x = 4

### Card 2
**Q:** General form of linear equation in one variable?
**A:** ax + b = 0

### Card 3
**Q:** Solution of ax + b = 0?
**A:** x = -b/a

### Card 4
**Q:** If 3x = 12, what is x?
**A:** 4

### Card 5
**Q:** What is the slope-intercept form?
**A:** y = mx + b

## Category 2: Quadratic Equations

### Card 6
**Q:** Standard form of quadratic?
**A:** ax² + bx + c = 0

### Card 7
**Q:** Quadratic formula?
**A:** x = (-b ± √(b²-4ac))/2a

### Card 8
**Q:** Discriminant formula?
**A:** Δ = b² - 4ac

### Card 9
**Q:** If Δ > 0, roots are?
**A:** Two distinct real roots

### Card 10
**Q:** If Δ = 0, roots are?
**A:** One repeated real root

### Card 11
**Q:** If Δ < 0, roots are?
**A:** Two complex conjugate roots

### Card 12
**Q:** Sum of roots = ?
**A:** -b/a

### Card 13
**Q:** Product of roots = ?
**A:** c/a

---

## Category 3: Factoring

### Card 14
**Q:** a² - b² = ?
**A:** (a + b)(a - b)

### Card 15
**Q:** a² + 2ab + b² = ?
**A:** (a + b)²

### Card 16
**Q:** a² - 2ab + b² = ?
**A:** (a - b)²

### Card 17
**Q:** a³ + b³ = ?
**A:** (a + b)(a² - ab + b²)

### Card 18
**Q:** a³ - b³ = ?
**A:** (a - b)(a² + ab + b²)

### Card 19
**Q:** Factor x² + 5x + 6
**A:** (x + 2)(x + 3)

### Card 20
**Q:** Factor x² - 4
**A:** (x + 2)(x - 2)

## Category 4: Polynomials

### Card 21
**Q:** Degree of 3x⁴ - 2x² + 1?
**A:** 4

### Card 22
**Q:** Remainder when P(x) divided by (x-a)?
**A:** P(a)

### Card 23

**A:** (x - a) is a factor of P(x)

**A:** P(a) = 0 ⟺ (x - a) is a factor

## Category 5: Sequences

### Card 26
**Q:** nth term of arithmetic sequence?
**A:** aₙ = a₁ + (n-1)d

### Card 27
**Q:** Sum of arithmetic series?
**A:** Sₙ = n(a₁ + aₙ)/2

### Card 28
**Q:** nth term of geometric sequence?
**A:** aₙ = a₁·rⁿ⁻¹

### Card 29
**Q:** Sum of geometric series (finite)?
**A:** Sₙ = a₁(1-rⁿ)/(1-r)

### Card 30
**Q:** Sum of infinite geometric series?
**A:** S∞ = a₁/(1-r), |r| < 1

## Category 6: Binomial Theorem

### Card 31
**Q:** (a + b)ⁿ = ?
**A:** Σₖ₌₀ⁿ C(n,k)aⁿ⁻ᵏbᵏ

### Card 32
**Q:** C(n,k) = ?
**A:** n!/[k!(n-k)!]

### Card 33
**Q:** Pascal's triangle identity?
**A:** C(n,k) = C(n-1,k-1) + C(n-1,k)

### Card 34
**Q:** Sum of all C(n,k) for k=0 to n?
**A:** 2ⁿ

### Card 35
**Q:** Middle term of (a+b)ⁿ when n even?
**A:** C(n, n/2)·aⁿ/²·bⁿ/²
