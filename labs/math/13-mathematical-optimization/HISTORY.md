# History of Mathematical Optimization

## Origins

### Early Developments
The foundations of mathematical optimization were laid by ancient mathematicians who first grappled with these concepts.

### 17th-18th Century
Major advances came during the scientific revolution as mathematics became formalized.

### 19th Century
Rigorous foundations were established, transforming mathematical optimization into a mature mathematical discipline.

### 20th Century
The field exploded with applications in computing, physics, and engineering.

### Modern Era
Today, mathematical optimization continues to evolve with new algorithms, computational methods, and cross-disciplinary applications.

## Key Contributors

Newton, Lagrange, Cauchy, Nesterov, Broyden-Fletcher-Goldfarb-Shanno

## Timeline

- Ancient: First recorded work on these concepts
- 1600s: Foundational developments
- 1700s: Formalization and extension
- 1800s: Rigorous framework established
- 1900s: Computational revolution
- 2000s: Modern algorithms and applications

## Impact on Computing

mathematical optimization has profoundly influenced computer science, enabling advances in cryptography, machine learning, scientific computing, data analysis, and artificial intelligence.
