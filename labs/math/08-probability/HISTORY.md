# History of Probability

## Timeline

| Year | Development |
|------|------------|
| 1654 | Pascal-Fermat correspondence on gambling problems |
| 1657 | Huygens: first published probability treatise |
| 1713 | Bernoulli: Law of Large Numbers |
| 1733 | De Moivre: normal distribution |
| 1763 | Bayes: posthumous essay on inverse probability |
| 1812 | Laplace: *Théorie Analytique des Probabilités* |
| 1933 | Kolmogorov: axiomatic probability theory |
| 1948 | Shannon: information theory (entropy) |
| 1980s | Bayesian revolution in statistics and ML |

## Key Figures

- **Blaise Pascal** (1623–1662) — foundation of probability
- **Pierre de Fermat** (1601–1665) — early probability theory
- **Pierre-Simon Laplace** (1749–1827) — Bayesian inference
- **Andrey Kolmogorov** (1903–1987) — modern axiomatic foundation
- **Claude Shannon** (1916–2001) — information theory
