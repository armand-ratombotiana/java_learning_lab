# Why Probability Exists

Probability was developed to reason about uncertainty, randomness, and chance.

## Core Motivations

- **Gambling**: odds, expected returns, fairness
- **Insurance**: risk assessment, premium calculation
- **Science**: experimental error, statistical inference
- **Weather**: forecasts, uncertainty quantification
- **Quantum mechanics**: fundamentally probabilistic nature of reality

Probability provides a mathematical language to describe and reason about the unpredictable.
