## Category 7: Important Theorems

### Card 31
**Q:** Fermat's Little Theorem states?
**A:** a^(p-1) ≡ 1 (mod p) for prime p, a not divisible by p

### Card 32
**Q:** Euclidean Algorithm finds?
**A:** gcd(a, b)

### Card 33
**Q:** Fundamental Theorem of Arithmetic?
**A:** Every integer > 1 has unique prime factorization

### Card 34
**Q:** Euler's Theorem?
**A:** a^φ(n) ≡ 1 (mod n) when gcd(a,n)=1

### Card 35
**Q:** Well-Ordering Principle?
**A:** Every non-empty subset of ℕ has a least element
