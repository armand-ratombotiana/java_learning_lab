# Why Arithmetic Exists

Arithmetic is the foundation of all quantitative reasoning. It exists because humans needed to count objects, trade goods, measure land, and track time. Every civilization independently developed arithmetic to solve practical problems.

Without arithmetic there would be no science, engineering, finance, or computing. The four basic operations form the bedrock upon which all higher mathematics is built.

## Core Purpose

- **Counting and inventory**: tracking resources
- **Trade and commerce**: pricing, profit, loss
- **Measurement**: length, area, volume, time
- **Computation**: the basis for all algorithms
