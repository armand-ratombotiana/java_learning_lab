# Why Trigonometry Exists

Trigonometry was developed for practical measurement problems where direct measurement was impossible.

## Origins

- **Astronomy**: measure distances to celestial bodies
- **Navigation**: determine position from stars
- **Surveying**: measure heights of mountains, depths of canyons
- **Architecture**: calculate structural loads at angles
- **Physics**: describe oscillations, waves, circular motion

The core need: given some angles and sides of a triangle, find the unknown ones — "triangle measuring" (trigon + metron).
