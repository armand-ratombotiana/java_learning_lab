# Why Calculus Exists

Calculus was invented to solve problems that algebra and geometry could not — problems involving continuous change.

## Core Motivations

- **Motion**: instantaneous velocity and acceleration
- **Optimization**: finding maximum area, minimum cost
- **Area**: area under curves (integration)
- **Physics**: laws of motion, electromagnetism, thermodynamics
- **Engineering**: stress analysis, fluid dynamics, control systems

Newton and Leibniz independently invented calculus in the 17th century to formalize the mathematics of change.
