# Why Combinatorics Exists

Combinatorics arose from humanity's need to count possibilities systematically.

## Core Motivations

- **Gambling**: odds of dice, cards, lotteries
- **Arrangements**: seating, scheduling, tournament brackets
- **Optimization**: finding the best arrangement among many
- **Probability**: counting favorable outcomes vs total outcomes
- **Computer science**: algorithm analysis (how many paths, how many comparisons)

Combinatorics provides the tools to answer "How many?" and "In how many ways?" — questions fundamental to decision-making under uncertainty.
