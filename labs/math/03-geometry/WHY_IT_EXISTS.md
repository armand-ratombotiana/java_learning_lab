# Why Geometry Exists

Geometry arises from the need to measure and understand physical space. Ancient Egyptians used geometry to re-survey land after Nile floods; Greeks elevated it to a deductive science.

## Core Motivations

- **Land measurement**: area, boundaries, property lines
- **Construction**: angles, load-bearing structures, arches
- **Navigation**: triangulation, celestial positioning
- **Art & design**: proportion, perspective, symmetry
- **Physics**: spacetime is geometric (general relativity)

Geometry provides the language to describe the physical world with precision.
