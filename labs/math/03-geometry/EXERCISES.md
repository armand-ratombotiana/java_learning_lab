# Exercises: Geometry

**1.** Find the area of a triangle with base 10 and height 6.
- Solution: Area = ½ × base × height = ½ × 10 × 6 = 30 square units

**2.** Calculate the circumference of a circle with radius 5.
- Solution: C = 2πr = 2π(5) = 10π

**3.** Find the volume of a cube with side length 4.
- Solution: V = s³ = 4³ = 64 cubic units

**4.** Calculate the distance between (2,3) and (6,7).
- Solution: d = √[(6-2)² + (7-3)²] = √(16+16) = √32 = 4√2

**5.** Find the area of a circle with diameter 10.
- Solution: r = 5, A = πr² = 25π

**6.** Calculate the surface area of a sphere with radius 3.
- Solution: SA = 4πr² = 4π(9) = 36π

**7.** Find the volume of a cone with radius 4 and height 6.
- Solution: V = ⅓πr²h = ⅓π(16)(6) = 32π

**8.** Calculate the diagonal of a rectangle with sides 5 and 12.
- Solution: d = √(5²+12²) = √(25+144) = √169 = 13

**9.** Find the area of an equilateral triangle with side 8.
- Solution: A = (s²√3)/4 = (64√3)/4 = 16√3

**10.** Calculate the slope of the line passing through (1,2) and (4,8).
- Solution: m = (8-2)/(4-1) = 6/3 = 2
