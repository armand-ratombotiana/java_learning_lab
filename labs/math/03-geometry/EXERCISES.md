# Exercises: Geometry

## Level 1: Basic Calculations

**1.** Find distance between points (3,4) and (7,1).
**2.** Find midpoint of (2,5) and (8,9).
**3.** Find area of triangle with vertices (0,0), (4,0), (0,3).
**4.** Find circumference of circle with radius 5.
**5.** Find area of circle with diameter 10.

## Level 2: Triangle Geometry

**6.** Find area of triangle with sides 3, 4, 5 using Heron's formula.
**7.** Find angles of triangle with vertices (0,0), (4,0), (2,3).
**8.** Find circumradius of equilateral triangle with side 6.
**9.** Find centroid of triangle with vertices (0,0), (6,0), (3,6).
**10.** Determine if triangle with sides 5, 12, 13 is right-angled.

## Level 3: Circle Problems

**11.** Find area of sector with radius 4 and angle π/3.
**12.** Find chord length subtending 60° at center with radius 6.
**13.** Find area of segment (minor) with radius 5 and angle 120°.
**14.** Two circles with radii 3 and 4 have distance 5 between centers. Find intersection points.
**15.** Find equation of circle through (1,1), (2,2), (3,1).

## Level 4: Quadrilaterals

**16.** Find area of trapezoid with parallel sides 4 and 8, height 3.
**17.** Find diagonal length of square with side 7.
**18.** Find area of rhombus with diagonals 6 and 8.
**19.** Find perimeter of rectangle with area 24 and length 6.
**20.** Determine if quadrilateral (0,0), (2,1), (1,3), (-1,2) is a parallelogram.

## Level 5: 3D Geometry

**21.** Find volume of sphere with radius 3.
**22.** Find surface area of cube with edge 5.
**23.** Find volume of cone with radius 4 and height 6.
**24.** Find surface area of cylinder with radius 3 and height 7.
**25.** Find volume of tetrahedron with vertices (0,0,0), (1,0,0), (0,1,0), (0,0,1).

## Level 6: Coordinate Geometry

**26.** Find equation of line through (2,3) and (5,7).
**27.** Find distance from point (3,4) to line 3x + 4y = 10.
**28.** Find angle between lines y = 2x + 1 and y = -x + 3.
**29.** Find foot of perpendicular from (3,4) to line y = 2x + 1.
**30.** Find reflection of (3,4) across line y = x.

## Level 7: Transformations

**31.** Find image of point (3,4) after rotation 90° about origin.
**32.** Find image of point (2,3) after scaling by factor 2 from origin.
**33.** Find transformation matrix for rotation by 45°.
**34.** Translate point (1,1) by vector (3,4).
**35.** Find composite transformation for rotation 90° followed by scaling 2.

## Level 8: Conic Sections

**36.** Find center and radius of circle x² + y² - 4x + 6y = 3.
**37.** Find foci of ellipse x²/16 + y²/9 = 1.
**38.** Find equation of parabola with focus (3,0) and directrix x = -3.
**39.** Find asymptotes of hyperbola x²/9 - y²/16 = 1.
**40.** Classify conic: 2x² + 3xy + y² = 5.

## Challenge Problems

**41.** Find area of polygon (0,0), (4,0), (5,3), (2,5), (-1,3).
**42.** Prove that medians divide triangle into 6 equal areas.
**43.** Find maximum area rectangle inscribed in a circle of radius R.
**44.** Find volume of solid formed by rotating y = x² from x=0 to x=1 about x-axis.
**45.** Given triangle with area A, find area of medial triangle (join midpoints).
