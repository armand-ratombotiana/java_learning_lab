## Category 3: Triangle Properties

### Card 11
**Q:** Pythagorean theorem?
**A:** a² + b² = c²

### Card 12
**Q:** Sum of angles in triangle?
**A:** 180°

### Card 13
**Q:** Area of equilateral triangle (side s)?
**A:** (√3/4)s²

### Card 14
**Q:** Heron's formula uses?
**A:** s = (a+b+c)/2, Area = √[s(s-a)(s-b)(s-c)]

### Card 15
**Q:** Law of sines?
**A:** sin(A)/a = sin(B)/b = sin(C)/c

### Card 16
**Q:** Law of cosines?

## Category 4: Coordinate Geometry

### Card 17
**Q:** Distance formula?
**A:** d = √[(x₂-x₁)² + (y₂-y₁)²]

### Card 18
**Q:** Midpoint formula?
**A:** M = ((x₁+x₂)/2, (y₁+y₂)/2)

### Card 19
**Q:** Slope formula?
**A:** m = (y₂-y₁)/(x₂-x₁)

### Card 20
**Q:** Point-slope form?
**A:** y - y₁ = m(x - x₁)
