# Why Statistics Exists

Statistics was developed to make sense of data — to separate signal from noise.

## Core Motivations

- **Census**: counting populations for taxation and representation
- **Epidemiology**: tracking disease outbreaks and mortality rates
- **Agriculture**: comparing crop yields with different treatments
- **Quality control**: manufacturing consistency
- **Social science**: understanding human behavior through data

Statistics provides tools to draw conclusions from incomplete data, quantify uncertainty, and make evidence-based decisions.
