# Flashcards: Multi-Tenancy

Card 1: Highest isolation pattern? A: Database-per-tenant
Card 2: Simplest pattern? A: Discriminator column
Card 3: Stores tenant ID per request? A: ThreadLocal
Card 4: Hibernate multi-tenancy interface? A: CurrentTenantIdentifierResolver
Card 5: Schema-per-tenant strategy? A: MultiTenancyStrategy.SCHEMA
Card 6: Tenant identification via DNS? A: Subdomain
Card 7: SaaS multi-tenancy pioneer? A: Salesforce
