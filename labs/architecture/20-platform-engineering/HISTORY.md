# History: Platform Engineering

## Origins
Platform engineering evolved from DevOps and infrastructure automation. The need for internal developer platforms emerged as organizations scaled their engineering teams.

## Evolution

### 2010-2015
DevOps movement emphasized automation and self-service. Organizations built custom internal tools.

### 2016
Spotify open-sourced Backstage, their developer portal. The concept of internal developer platforms gained visibility.

### 2018
Platform engineering emerged as a distinct discipline. Team topologies book formalized platform team patterns.

### 2020
Backstage entered CNCF incubation. Golden path concept formalized by ThoughtWorks.

### 2022
Platform engineering becomes a recognized role. IDP products and frameworks proliferate.

## Key Milestones
- 2016: Backstage open-sourced
- 2018: Team Topologies published
- 2020: Backstage CNCF incubation
- 2021: Golden Paths by ThoughtWorks
- 2022: Platform engineering as discipline
