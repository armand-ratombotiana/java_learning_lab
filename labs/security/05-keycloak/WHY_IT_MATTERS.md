# Why 05-keycloak Matters

## Business Impact

### Financial Consequences

Security breaches have massive financial implications:
- Average cost of a data breach: $4.45 million (IBM 2023)
- Regulatory fines for non-compliance can reach 4% of global revenue
- Customer churn and reputation damage have long-term costs

### Regulatory Compliance

Understanding Keycloak setup, realms, clients, users, roles, identity brokering is essential for compliance with:
- **GDPR** - General Data Protection Regulation
- **HIPAA** - Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act
- **PCI-DSS** - Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard
- **SOX** - Sarbanes-Oxley Act

### Customer Trust

Security is a fundamental component of customer trust.

## Technical Impact

Mastering Keycloak setup, realms, clients, users, roles, identity brokering means working with industry standards that are widely adopted and continuously improved.

### Security Posture

Proper implementation of Keycloak setup, realms, clients, users, roles, identity brokering significantly improves an application's overall security posture.
