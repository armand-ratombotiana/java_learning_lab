# Why 09-authorization-rbac Matters

## Business Impact

### Financial Consequences

Security breaches have massive financial implications:
- Average cost of a data breach: $4.45 million (IBM 2023)
- Regulatory fines for non-compliance can reach 4% of global revenue
- Customer churn and reputation damage have long-term costs

### Regulatory Compliance

Understanding Role-based access control, permissions, ACL, method security is essential for compliance with:
- **GDPR** - General Data Protection Regulation
- **HIPAA** - Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act
- **PCI-DSS** - Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard
- **SOX** - Sarbanes-Oxley Act

### Customer Trust

Security is a fundamental component of customer trust.

## Technical Impact

### Industry Standards

Mastering Role-based access control, permissions, ACL, method security means working with industry standards that are widely adopted and continuously improved.

### Security Posture

Proper implementation of Role-based access control, permissions, ACL, method security significantly improves an application's overall security posture.

### Audit Readiness

Well-implemented security makes auditing and compliance verification straightforward.

## Java/Spring Advantage

Spring Security's battle-tested implementations of Role-based access control, permissions, ACL, method security reduce the risk of implementation errors.
