# Flashcards — Online Algorithms

Q: Competitive ratio?
A: Worst-case ratio of online vs optimal offline cost

Q: LRU competitive ratio?
A: k (cache size), optimal deterministic

Q: Belady's anomaly?
A: More cache can increase FIFO faults

Q: Ski rental deterministic ratio?
A: 2 - 1/B

Q: Secretary problem success rate?
A: 1/e =~ 37%

Q: Epsilon-greedy?
A: Explore with probability epsilon, exploit with 1-epsilon

Q: Optimal offline paging?
A: Evict page used furthest in future (Belady's MIN)

Q: Regret?
A: Cumulative difference between chosen arm and optimal arm rewards
