# Flashcards — Matrix Algorithms

Q: Strassen complexity?
A: O(n^{log_2 7}) ≈ O(n^{2.807})

Q: Strassen uses how many multiplications?
A: 7 (instead of 8)

Q: Gaussian elimination complexity?
A: O(n^3)

Q: Partial pivoting?
A: Select row with largest |A[i][k]| as pivot

Q: LU decomposition?
A: P * A = L * U

Q: How LU solves Ax = b?
A: Forward substitute Ly = Pb, back substitute Ux = y

Q: SVD?
A: A = U * Sigma * V^T

Q: Power iteration converges to?
A: Dominant eigenvalue and eigenvector
