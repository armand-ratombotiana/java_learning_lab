# Flashcards — Quantum Simulation

Q: What is a qubit?
A: Two-level quantum system with complex amplitudes

Q: Hadamard gate output on |0>?
A: (|0> + |1>) / sqrt(2)

Q: Measurement probability?
A: |amplitude|^2

Q: Grover iteration count?
A: floor(pi/4 * sqrt(N))

Q: QFT complexity?
A: O(n^2) gates for n qubits

Q: CNOT?
A: Controlled NOT: flips target if control is |1>

Q: Unitary matrix?
A: U^dagger U = I

Q: Tensor product?
A: kronecker product combines multi-qubit states
