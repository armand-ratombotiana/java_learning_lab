# WebFlux Deep Dive -- Interview Questions
Q1: What is the Reactive Manifesto?
A: Responsive, Resilient, Elastic, Message-Driven
Q2: Flux vs Mono?
A: Flux emits 0-N items, Mono emits 0-1 items
Q3: What is backpressure?
A: Flow control mechanism between producer and consumer
Q4: Hot vs Cold publisher?
A: Cold creates per-subscriber stream; Hot shares one stream
Q5: What is StepVerifier?
A: Testing utility for reactive streams
