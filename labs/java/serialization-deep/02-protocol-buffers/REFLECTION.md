# Protocol Buffers -- Reflection

## Guided Self-Reflection Prompts

### Understanding Check
1. Can you explain how serialization works to a junior developer in 3 sentences?
2. What is the most surprising thing you learned about serialization?
3. What mental model do you find most useful for understanding serialization?

### Practical Application
4. In your current or past projects, how was serialization used?
5. What serialization format would you choose for a new microservice and why?
6. How would you design a version-tolerant serialization schema?

### Problem Solving
7. How would you debug a serialization issue in production?
8. What steps would you take to secure deserialization in your application?
9. How would you optimize serialization for a high-throughput system?

### Design Decisions
10. When would you choose Java serialization over JSON or Protobuf?
11. When would you implement Externalizable instead of Serializable?
12. What factors influence your choice of serialization framework?

### Code Review
13. Review a past serialization implementation. What would you do differently?
14. What serialization anti-patterns have you encountered or created?
15. How would you improve the serialization design in a codebase you know?

### Future Learning
16. What aspect of serialization do you want to learn more about?
17. How does serialization relate to other concepts like reflection and I/O?
18. What current trends are shaping the future of serialization in Java?

### Knowledge Gaps
19. What questions about serialization do you still have?
20. What would you need to practice to feel confident with serialization?
