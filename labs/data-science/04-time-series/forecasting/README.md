# Time Series Forecasting

Welcome to the atomic mastery lab for **Time Series Forecasting**. This lab is part of the Data Science Academy's Time Series module.

## 🧠 What You Will Master
- The unique challenges of Time Series data (Temporal Dependence).
- Components of a Time Series (Trend, Seasonality, Residuals).
- The concept of Stationarity and Differencing.
- Autocorrelation and Partial Autocorrelation.
- The ARIMA model intuition (Auto-Regressive, Integrated, Moving Average).
- Implementing a basic Exponential Smoothing model in Java.

## 📂 Lab Structure
1. [THEORY.md](./THEORY.md) - The components of time series and stationarity.
2. [MATH_FOUNDATION.md](./MATH_FOUNDATION.md) - Autocorrelation and ARIMA equations.
3. [CODE_DEEP_DIVE.md](./CODE_DEEP_DIVE.md) - Pure Java implementation of Exponential Smoothing.
