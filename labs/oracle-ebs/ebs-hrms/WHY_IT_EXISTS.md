# Why It Exists: EBS HRMS

## 1. Business Need

Before EBS, companies used siloed applications. EBS HRMS solves fragmentation. Single source of truth.

## 2. Design Philosophy

EBS designed for configurability over customization. Configure via profiles, flexfields, workflows.

## 3. Competitive Landscape

EBS competes with SAP, Dynamics, Infor. Advantage: Oracle technology stack integration.

## 4. Standards Compliance

GAAP/IFRS, SOX, GDPR, localization requirements.

## 5. Why Not Custom?

Custom build requires thousands of person-years. Less functionality, worse integration, higher costs.
