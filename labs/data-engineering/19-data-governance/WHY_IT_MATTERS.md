# Why Data Governance Matters

- **Compliance**: Meet regulatory requirements (GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, SOX)
- **Trust**: Protect customer data and maintain brand reputation
- **Risk Reduction**: Minimize breach impact through least-privilege access
- **Data Value**: Well-governed data is trusted and usable
- **Operational Efficiency**: Automated policies reduce manual overhead
- **Auditability**: Clear trails for compliance and incident investigation
