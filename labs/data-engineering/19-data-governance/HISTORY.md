# History of Data Governance

## Timeline
- **1970s**: Data administration emerges as formal discipline
- **1990s**: Data governance frameworks (DGI, CMMI)
- **2000s**: Sarbanes-Oxley (SOX) drives governance technology
- **2014**: EU Data Protection Regulation drafted (GDPR precursor)
- **2018**: GDPR goes into effect; major compliance push
- **2020**: CCPA in California; global privacy regulation wave
- **2023**: AI governance emerges; data governance for ML/AI pipelines
- **2024**: Automated governance; AI-driven policy enforcement
