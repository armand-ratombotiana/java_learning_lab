# Flashcards: Data Governance

## Card 1
**Front**: What is data governance?
**Back**: Framework of policies, processes, and technologies for managing data availability, usability, integrity, and security

## Card 2
**Front**: What is RBAC?
**Back**: Role-Based Access Control: permissions assigned to roles, users assigned to roles, role hierarchy for inheritance

## Card 3
**Front**: What is PII?
**Back**: Personally Identifiable Information (name, SSN, email, phone, address) that can identify an individual

## Card 4
**Front**: What is data masking?
**Back**: Techniques to obfuscate sensitive data: substitution, shuffling, nulling, encryption, format-preserving masking

## Card 5
**Front**: What is GDPR?
**Back**: EU General Data Protection Regulation: right to access, rectification, erasure, portability, and restriction of processing
