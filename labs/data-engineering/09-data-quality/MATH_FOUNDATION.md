# Math Foundation

## Statistical
`Z-Score = (xi - mean) / stddev`
`Outlier: |Z| > 3 (99.7% confidence)`
`IQR = Q3 - Q1`
`Outlier: x < Q1 - 1.5*IQR or x > Q3 + 1.5*IQR`

## Quality Metrics
`Completeness = NonNull / Total * 100`
`Uniqueness = Distinct / Total * 100`
`Overall DQ Score = WeightedAverage(dimension_scores)`
