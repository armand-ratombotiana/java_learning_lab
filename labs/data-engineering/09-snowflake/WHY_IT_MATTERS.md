# Why Snowflake Data Cloud Matters

- **Cost Efficiency**: Pay only for storage (cheap) and compute (only when running)
- **Concurrency**: Multiple warehouses query same data without contention
- **Simplicity**: No indexing, partitioning, or manual tuning required
- **Data Sharing**: Instant, secure sharing without copying data
- **Ecosystem**: Integrates with all major BI tools, ETL platforms, and ML frameworks
- **Cloud Agnostic**: Consistent experience across AWS, Azure, and GCP
