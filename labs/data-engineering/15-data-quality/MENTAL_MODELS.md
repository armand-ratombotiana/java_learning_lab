# Mental Models for Data Quality Engineering

## 1. The Building Inspection
Quality checks are like building inspections. Before occupancy (data consumption), verify foundation (schema), wiring (relationships), plumbing (nulls), and permits (format validation).

## 2. The Factory QC
Quality checks are factory quality control stations. Each station checks a dimension. Failed items are flagged, quarantined, or reworked. Trend charts show quality over production runs.

## 3. The Health Checkup
Data quality monitoring is like regular health checkups. Vital signs (metrics) are tracked over time. Anomalies (spikes in failure rates) trigger alerts for investigation.
