# History of Delta Lake

## Timeline
- **2017**: Developed internally at Databricks
- **2019**: Open-sourced under Linux Foundation
- **2020**: Delta Lake 0.8 with Delta Sharing
- **2021**: Delta Lake 1.0 GA; change data feed
- **2022**: Delta Lake 2.0; Delta Sharing open-sourced
- **2023**: Delta Lake 3.0; UniForm (Iceberg compatibility)
- **2024**: Delta Lake 3.2; liquid clustering, identity columns
