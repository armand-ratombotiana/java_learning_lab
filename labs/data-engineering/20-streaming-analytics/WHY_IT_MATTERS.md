# Why Streaming Analytics Matters

- **Real-Time Decisions**: Act on data as it arrives, not hours later
- **Operational Awareness**: Live visibility into system and business health
- **Customer Experience**: Detect and respond to issues before users notice
- **Competitive Advantage**: Faster insights enable faster responses
- **Cost Efficiency**: Process only what's needed, not full daily reprocessing
- **Scalability**: Handle increasing event volumes without batch window constraints
