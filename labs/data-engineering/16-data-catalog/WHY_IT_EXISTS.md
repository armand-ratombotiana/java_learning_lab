# Why Data Catalog Exists

Modern organizations have hundreds or thousands of datasets across databases, data lakes, streaming platforms, and BI tools. Without a catalog, finding the right dataset takes hours or days. Data lineage is unknown. Ownership is unclear. Metadata is scattered across wikis, spreadsheets, and READMEs. A data catalog centralizes metadata, enables discovery, and provides the foundation for data governance.
