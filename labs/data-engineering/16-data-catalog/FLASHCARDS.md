# Flashcards: Data Catalog

## Card 1
**Front**: What is a data catalog?
**Back**: Centralized metadata platform for data discovery, governance, lineage, and collaboration

## Card 2
**Front**: What is data lineage?
**Back**: End-to-end tracking of data flow: origin, transformations, and consumption points

## Card 3
**Front**: What is Apache Atlas?
**Back**: Open-source data governance platform for Hadoop ecosystem with lineage and classification

## Card 4
**Front**: What is OpenMetadata?
**Back**: Modern open-source metadata platform with active community and extensive connector ecosystem

## Card 5
**Front**: What is a business glossary?
**Back**: Curated collection of business terms, definitions, and relationships for consistent data understanding
