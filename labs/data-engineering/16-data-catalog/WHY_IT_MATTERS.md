# Why Data Catalog Matters

- **Productivity**: Reduce time finding data from hours to seconds
- **Trust**: Verified metadata, ownership, and quality scores build confidence
- **Governance**: Track data lineage, enforce classification, manage changes
- **Collaboration**: Shared business glossary aligns data and business teams
- **Compliance**: Identify and track sensitive data for regulatory requirements
- **Impact Analysis**: Before changing a source, see all downstream dependencies
