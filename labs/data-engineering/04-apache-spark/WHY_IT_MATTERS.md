- **Speed**: 100x faster than Hadoop MR for iterative algorithms

- **Ecosystem**: Integrates with Kafka, HBase, Cassandra, S3
- **Adoption**: 80%+ Fortune 500 companies

## Performance
- Process 100TB+ datasets
- Stream millions of events/sec
