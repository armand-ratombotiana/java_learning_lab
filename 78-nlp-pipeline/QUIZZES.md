# NLP Pipeline Quizzes

## Easy Questions

1. What is tokenization? (Breaking text into tokens)
2. What does POS stand for? (Part of Speech)
3. What is stemming? (Reducing words to root form)
4. What is the Bag of Words model? (Text representation)
5. What is TF-IDF? (Term frequency-inverse document frequency)

## Medium Questions

6. What is lemmatization? (Words to dictionary form)
7. What are stopwords? (Common words to remove)
8. How does Stanford CoreNLP work? (Pipeline of annotators)
9. What is extractive summarization? (Selecting key sentences)
10. What is named entity recognition? (Finding entities in text)

## Advanced Questions

11. What is the difference between extractive and abstractive summarization?
12. How does LDA topic modeling work? (Latent Dirichlet allocation)
13. What are the challenges in sentiment analysis?
14. How do you build a custom NLP pipeline?
15. What are the best practices for feature selection in NLP?

## Answers

1. Breaking text into tokens
2. Part of Speech
3. Reducing words to root form
4. Text representation based on word counts
5. Term frequency-inverse document frequency
6. Words to dictionary form
7. Common words filtered out
8. Pipeline of annotators
9. Selecting key sentences from text
10. Finding entities (person, org, location) in text
